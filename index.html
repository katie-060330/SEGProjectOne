Welcome to Minto Sport Complex

As the proud home of the Gee Gee sporting community, we are here for all uOttawa students and staff's sporting needs. Whether dropping your kid off for camp and getting a quick workout in, participating in our drop-in program to get some friendly competition, or even better yourself by signing up for one of our programs with our members of our team. We have something for everyone of all ages; book now!
